HKDSE English Language Paper 1 (Reading) — Part A

以下為全部 15 題參考答案及詳細解釋。開放式題目可接受意思相近的答案。

Total marks: 20 marks

1 Short Answer 1 mark

Suggested answer: They believe individual small daily actions cannot solve large global environmental problems.

Paragraph 1 states teenagers think "single personal choices cannot fix global environmental crises."

青少年認為個人小行動無法解決全球環境危機。

[1] Many teenagers think single personal choices cannot fix global environmental crises...

2 Multiple Choice 1 mark

Answer: A — energy-wasting standby electrical machines

"Vampire loads" refers to electrical devices that secretly consume electricity on standby mode.

「Vampire loads」指待機模式下持續耗電的電器。

3 Short Answer 1 mark

Suggested answer: Switch off unused electrical devices fully; hang clothes outdoors to dry without dryers.

Paragraph 2 suggests: switch devices off from wall sockets and air-dry clothes.

第2段建議：完全關掉電器；室外晾衣代替乾衣機。

[2] Students can switch all chargers... off fully from wall sockets... hang washed clothes outside to air-dry...

4 True / False / Not Given 3 marks

(i) Not Given   (ii) True   (iii) False

(i) No comparison of showers vs baths. (ii) Carpooling "cut the number of separate vehicles." (iii) Plastic takes "hundreds of years."

(i) 未提及 (ii) 真 (iii) 假——塑膠需要數百年才能分解。

5 Short Answer 1 mark

Suggested answer: Disposable water bottles, plastic snack wrappers, single-use shopping bags

Paragraph 5 lists these three items.

第5段列舉：即棄水樽、零食包裝袋、購物膠袋。

[5] Disposable water bottles, snack wrappers and shopping bags...

6 Short Answer 1 mark

Suggested answer: Low-flow water taps in school washrooms

Paragraph 3: "install low-flow water taps in washrooms."

安裝低流量水龍頭。

[3] Schools can also install low-flow water taps in washrooms...

7 Find Word 1 mark

Answer: reusable

Paragraph 5: "carry reusable metal bottles and cloth bags."

"Reusable" 指可重複使用。

[5] Students can carry reusable metal bottles and cloth bags every day...

8 Summary — ONE word each 4 marks

(i) carbon emissions   (ii) clean-up   (iii) effects   (iv) heating

(i) para 2 (ii) para 5 (iii) para 6 (iv) para 7

各詞均須出自文中。

9 Short Answer 1 mark

Suggested answer: Walking, riding bicycles, taking public transport (buses / MTR)

Paragraph 4 lists these options.

第4段列出：步行、騎單車、乘搭公共巴士或港鐵。

[4] Teenagers can choose walking, cycling or public buses and MTR trains...

10 Difference & Similarity 2 marks

(i) Difference: Cloth bags can be reused many times; plastic bags are single-use waste.
(ii) Similarity: Both are used to carry shopping goods.

Difference from para 5. Similarity: both carry items.

差異：布袋可重複使用，膠袋即棄。相似：兩者都用來攜帶物品。

11 Short Answer 1 mark

Suggested answer: Talk about eco tips with family and peers; give class environmental presentations; join school green clubs.

Paragraph 6 lists three ways.

第6段列出三種方式。

[6] Share eco tips with family members and classmates... give short student presentations... support youth environmental clubs at school.

12 Short Answer 1 mark

Suggested answer: Buy unpackaged fresh fruit and vegetables instead of wrapped processed snacks.

Paragraph 5: "select unpackaged fresh fruit and vegetables."

選擇無包裝的新鮮蔬果。

[5] ...select unpackaged fresh fruit and vegetables from markets instead of pre-wrapped processed snacks.

13 Short Answer 1 mark

Suggested answer: They secretly waste electricity constantly even when people think they are turned off.

Paragraph 2: "secretly consume constant electricity" like vampires.

待機模式下的電器像吸血鬼一樣暗中持續耗電。

[2] Household electrical devices left on standby mode secretly consume constant electricity...

14 Multiple Choice 1 mark

Answer: B — Generally hopeful

The writer strongly believes in youth power: "youth power is far stronger than many imagine."

作者堅信青年力量，語氣鼓勵且樂觀。

[7] ...youth power is far stronger than many imagine.

15 Short Answer 1 mark

Suggested answer: Big global environmental problems can be improved by lots of small daily sustainable choices made by young people.

The title emphasises "Small" and "Teen" — small actions by young people can make a difference.

標題強調小行動和青少年也能帶來改變。
