1 Short Answer 1 mark

Suggested answer: They grow thick, long black fur that traps heat against cold mountain air.

Paragraph 1: thick long black fur helps them survive cold mountain nights near freezing.

厚長黑毛幫助牠們在接近冰點的山區夜晚保暖。

[1] These large primates have thick long black fur to survive cold mountain nights...

2 Multiple Choice 1 mark

Answer: B — adult male gorilla with grey back fur

Silverbacks are mature males with grey silver fur on their backs, appearing around age 12.

Silverback 指背部长出銀灰色毛的成年雄性大猩猩。

[2] Silverbacks get their name from the grey silver fur that grows across their backs once they reach twelve years old.

3 Short Answer 1 mark

Suggested answer: He protects all troop members from predators and competing gorilla groups.

Paragraph 2: the troop leader protects all females, young gorillas and babies from leopards and rival groups.

首領保護所有群體成員免受豹子和競爭群體的傷害。

[2] The troop leader protects all females, young gorillas and babies from leopards and rival gorilla groups.

4 True / False / Not Given 1 mark

(i) False (ii) True (iii) False

(i) False — they eat over 140 kinds of plants. (ii) True — para 3: 'calm and peaceful.' (iii) False — para 5: watch from 10-metre distance.

(i) 假——吃超過140種植物 (ii) 真——冷靜溫和 (iii) 假——需保持10米距離。

5 Short Answer 1 mark

Suggested answer: Clearing forest for farmland; illegal poaching for wildlife black markets; human settlement inside gorilla habitats.

Paragraph 4 lists three pressures: deforestation, poaching, and civil conflict.

第4段列出三項壓力：森林砍伐、盜獵、內戰。

[4] Farmers cut down cloud forest trees... Illegal poachers hunt gorillas... Civil conflict... forces local families to move into gorilla habitats.

6 Short Answer 1 mark

Suggested answer: They build leaf nests on tree branches or the thick forest ground and sleep together as a troop.

Paragraph 2: all gorillas sleep together in leaf nests.

所有大猩猩一起在樹枝或地面的葉巢中睡覺。

[2] ...all gorillas sleep together in leaf nests built on tree branches or thick forest ground every evening.

7 Find Word 1 mark

Answer: patrols

Paragraph 5: 'Anti-poaching patrol teams carry daily forest walks.'

第5段：反盜獵巡邏隊。

[5] Anti-poaching patrol teams carry daily forest walks to stop illegal hunters...

8 Summary — ONE word each 1 mark

(i) rangers (ii) ecosystem (iii) territory (iv) loss

(i) para 6: 'patrol rangers' (ii) para 7: 'cloud forest ecosystem' (iii) para 5: 'wild gorilla territory' (iv) para 6: 'habitat loss'

各詞均須出自文中。

9 Short Answer 1 mark

Suggested answer: Daily anti-poaching forest patrols; regulated safe-distance eco-tourism tours; border fences to stop farm expansion.

Paragraph 5 lists three cross-border measures.

第5段列出三項跨境保育措施。

[5] Anti-poaching patrol teams... Eco-tourism programmes... National park borders are marked with clear fences...

10 Difference & Similarity 1 mark

(i) Difference: Silverbacks grow grey silver fur on their backs; baby gorillas have full black fur only. (ii) Similarity: Both live within a single troop and eat forest plants.

Difference from para 2. Similarity: both live in troops and eat plants.

差異：銀背有灰色毛，幼猩只有黑毛。相似：都生活在群體中吃植物。

11 Short Answer 1 mark

Suggested answer: All tour money pays for forest wildlife protection and local village school development.

Paragraph 5: tour fees fund forest protection and local village schools.

旅遊收入用於森林保護和當地鄉村學校。

[5] ...all tour fees fund forest protection and local village schools.

12 Short Answer 1 mark

Suggested answer: They work as wildlife eco-tour guides or park anti-poaching rangers.

Paragraph 6: former poachers become wildlife guides or patrol rangers.

前盜獵者成為生態導遊或巡邏管理員。

[6] Many former poachers now work as wildlife guides or patrol rangers...

13 Short Answer 1 mark

Suggested answer: They carry fruit seeds in their waste as they travel, scattering seeds across cleared mountain slopes.

Paragraph 7: gorillas spread seeds through their waste, helping plants grow.

大猩猩通過糞便傳播種子，幫助植物生長。

[7] ...they spread fruit seeds through their waste, helping hundreds of rare forest plant species grow...

14 Multiple Choice 1 mark

Answer: B — Generally hopeful

The writer highlights successful conservation projects and population recovery, showing hope.

作者強調成功的保育項目和數量恢復，態度樂觀。

15 Short Answer 1 mark

Suggested answer: Mountain gorillas are very large, peaceful primates that live in cold misty highland African rainforests.

The title describes them as 'gentle giants' living in 'African cloud forests'.

標題描述牠們為生活在非洲雲霧森林的溫和巨人。
