1 Answer 1 mark

Bioluminescence

Paragraph 1: bioluminescence is the scientific term for fireflies' natural glowing light.

Bioluminescence 是螢火蟲自然發光的科學術語。

2 Answer 1 mark

A — natural living things creating their own light

Bioluminescence means living things creating their own light.

生物發光指生物自行發光。

3 Answer 1 mark

Luciferin and luciferase

Paragraph 2: these two special chemicals inside the firefly's abdomen create the glowing flash.

螢光素和螢光素酶是腹部內產生閃光的兩種化學物質。

4 Answer 1 mark

(i) False (ii) True (iii) False

(i) False — firefly light produces zero waste heat. (ii) True — para 2: each species has a unique flashing pattern. (iii) False — adult fireflies live only 2-4 weeks.

Their bodies contain bitter toxic chemicals that taste unpleasant to predators.

Paragraph 3: the constant faint glow warns predators of bitter poison inside their bodies.

持續的微光警告捕食者體內含有苦味毒素。
