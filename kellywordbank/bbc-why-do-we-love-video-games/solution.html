1 Short Answer 1 mark

(i) the film industry   (ii) the music industry

Directly from line 1: "This huge worldwide industry brings in over 200 billion US dollars every year, out-earning both the film and music businesses combined."

第一行直接提取：遊戲產業賺錢比電影業和音樂業加起來還多，所以這兩個賺得更少。

[1] ...This huge worldwide industry brings in over 200 billion US dollars every year, out-earning both the film and music businesses combined.

2 Vocabulary 1 mark

Answer: B — meaningless

"Critics often claim gaming is a pointless waste of time" → critics say gaming has no purpose/meaning, so closest meaning is "meaningless".

批評者說打遊戲是「pointless waste of time」，pointless 就是沒有意義，所以選B meaningless。

[1] Critics often claim gaming is a pointless waste of time...

3 T/F/NG 3 marks

i) F   ii) F   iii) T

i) False → "average gamer is 35 years old, and almost half of players are women" → most are NOT teenage boys → false.
ii) False → "Unlike passive activities such as watching TV, gamers must make constant active choices" → gaming requires MORE active decision-making than TV, NOT the other way around → false.
iii) True → "Studies from university labs found that regular gamers perform better at logical reasoning tests than people who rarely play" → matches statement → true.

i) 錯——文中說平均玩家35歲，接近一半是女性，所以大部分不是十幾歲男生，錯。
ii) 錯——文中說看電視是被動活動，打遊戲玩家要不斷主動選擇，所以打遊戲需要更多主動決策，和聲明相反，錯。
iii) 對——大學實驗室研究發現經常打遊戲的人邏輯推理測試表現更好，符合聲明，對。

[1] Recent surveys show that the average gamer in the UK is 35 years old, and almost half of players are women. [2] Unlike passive activities such watching TV, gamers must make constant active choices to progress through levels. Studies from university labs found that regular gamers perform better at logical reasoning tests than people who rarely play.

4 Reference 1 mark

It refers to playing video games / gaming.

Previous sentence: "Gaming also builds social connections, even though many outsiders see it as a lonely hobby." → "it" and "this hobby" both refer to gaming/playing video games.

前一句：「Gaming also builds social connections, even though many outsiders see it as a lonely hobby.」，所以 this hobby 就是指打遊戲/遊戲這個愛好。

[3] Gaming also builds social connections, even though many outsiders see it as a lonely hobby.

5 Short Answer 2 marks

(Any two of these):
1. They can connect and play with friends even when they live far apart
2. Virtual worlds offer a low-pressure environment without awkward face-to-face conversations
3. They can meet new people in a comfortable low-stress way

Paragraph 3 says: online multiplayer lets friends cooperate from far away, and for shy people it's low-pressure without awkward in-person chats.

第三段說：線上多人遊戲讓朋友即使住很遠也可以一起玩，而且對於害羞的人，虛擬世界提供低壓力環境，不需要尷尬面對面聊天，所以更容易交朋友。

[3] Online multiplayer modes let friends talk and cooperate on shared missions, no matter how far apart they live. For shy or socially anxious people, virtual worlds offer a low-pressure space to make new pals without awkward face-to-face conversations.

clans

Directly from text: "Many lifelong friendships start from online gaming teams, known as "clans"."

文中直接說：許多終生友誼從線上遊戲隊伍開始，這些隊伍叫做 clans。

[3] Many lifelong friendships start from online gaming teams, known as "clans".

8 Listing 3 marks

(1) eating meals (they skip meals)
(2) sleeping (they cut sleep short)
(3) real-life responsibilities

Directly from paragraph 4: "Some players develop unhealthy addictive habits: they skip meals, cut sleep short and ignore real-life responsibilities to keep playing." All three required.

第四段直接列出三樣：成癮玩家為了繼續玩，會不吃飯、壓縮睡眠時間、忽略現實生活責任。

[4] Some players develop unhealthy addictive habits: they skip meals, cut sleep short and ignore real-life responsibilities to keep playing.

9 Short Answer 3 marks

To encourage players to keep coming back and play for longer sessions / to keep players hooked and coming back regularly to the game.

Text: "Game designers intentionally add reward loops, such as daily login prizes and rare item drops, to keep users coming back for longer sessions."

遊戲設計師故意加入這些獎勵循環，讓玩家不斷回來玩更長時間，讓玩家上癮。

[4] Game designers intentionally add reward loops, such as daily login prizes and rare item drops, to keep users coming back for longer sessions.

10 Inference 2 marks

The common myth challenged is the wrong belief that all video games make players become violent in real life / playing violent video games causes real-world aggressive behaviour.

Text: "There is also a common myth that all video games make players violent. Research carried out over 15 years found no reliable long-term link between game violence and real-world aggressive behaviour." → this myth is challenged by research, so it is the wrong belief.

第五段挑戰大眾錯誤迷思：「所有電子遊戲都會令玩家在現實中變得暴力」，研究發現沒有可靠證據顯示遊戲暴力和現實攻擊行為有長期聯繫，所以這個錯誤信念被挑戰。

[5] There is also a common myth that all video games make players violent. Research carried out over 15 years found no reliable long-term link between game violence and real-world aggressive behaviour.

11 Difference & Similarity 2 marks

i) Difference: Playing action games improves players' hand-eye coordination and reaction speed, while watching violent films does not.
ii) Similarity: Both action games and violent films contain violent content/images.

The myth is "all video games make players violent" → the similarity is that both contain violence. The difference is that unlike passive watching, action games improve player reaction and coordination skills.

差異：玩動作遊戲會提升玩家手眼協調和反應速度，而看暴力電影不會。
相似：動作遊戲和暴力電影都包含暴力內容。

[5] Instead, playing action games can improve hand-eye coordination and split-second reaction speeds, skills useful for jobs like surgery or piloting aircraft.

12 Short Answer 2 marks

(i) surgeons (surgery)
(ii) aircraft pilots (piloting aircraft)

Directly from text: "skills useful for jobs like surgery or piloting aircraft."

文中直接說：這些快速反應技能對外科醫生和飛機飛行員的工作很有用。

[5] ...playing action games can improve hand-eye coordination and split-second reaction speeds, skills useful for jobs like surgery or piloting aircraft.

13 Phrase Meaning 3 marks

It suggests that gaming has both benefits and risks, so people need to control their playing time and play moderately, avoid extreme addiction, while still enjoying the benefits.

"Strike a healthy balance" means you don't have to quit gaming entirely, but you shouldn't play too much either. You need to balance gaming with real-life responsibilities and family time.

「strike a healthy balance」意思就是：打遊戲有好處也有風險，所以人們需要控制玩的時間，適度玩樂，避免極端成癮，同時享受好處，平衡遊戲和現實生活責任。

[6] To strike a healthy balance, child psychologists suggest simple rules for all gamers. Set fixed daily time limits for play...

14 Attitude 1 mark

Answer: B — Positive if played in reasonable amounts

The writer explains mental benefits of gaming, also warns about addiction risks, and concludes: "When enjoyed moderately, video games can be a valuable hobby that trains the brain and strengthens friendships." → this matches B exactly.

作者解釋了打遊戲的心智好處，也警告成癮風險，結論說：「當適度享受時，電子遊戲可以是訓練大腦加強友誼的有價值愛好」，所以B正確：如果合理份量玩就是正面態度。

[6] When enjoyed moderately, video games can be a valuable hobby that trains the brain and strengthens friendships.

15 Inference 2 marks

It implies that many people in the public only see the negative downsides of gaming (addiction, violence, waste of time) and overlook or don't understand the positive mental and social benefits that gaming can offer.

The title asks "why do we love it" → this suggests that most people don't understand why people love gaming because they only see the negatives, so the article explains the hidden benefits.

標題問「我們為什麼喜歡電子遊戲？」，暗示公眾一般只看到遊戲的負面缺點（成癮、暴力、浪費時間），而忽略或不理解遊戲可以帶來的正面心智和社交好處，所以文章解釋這些被忽視的好處。

Title: Why do we love video games? [1] Critics often claim gaming is a pointless waste of time, yet psychologists argue that playing games offers surprising mental benefits.
