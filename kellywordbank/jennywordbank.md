### boost  (v/n) <-- Added on 2026-07-09
文章分類：general-vocab
解釋: 促進；提高；推動；增強

**[深度解析 & 語境應用]**
英文定義: To increase or improve something; an increase or improvement.
重點記憶點: DSE常見：boost economy 促進經濟，boost confidence 增強信心。

B1 / B2 (例句):The new policy should help boost economic growth in the region.
詞組搭配 (Collocations): boost economy, boost confidence, boost sales

同義詞 (Synonyms): increase, improve, promote
反義詞 (Antonyms): reduce, decrease, hinder
