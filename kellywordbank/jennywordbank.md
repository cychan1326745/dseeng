### mass-produced  (adj) <-- Added on 2026-07-09
文章分類：general
解釋: 批量生產的；大量生產的

**[深度解析 & 語境應用]**
英文定義: Produced in large quantities by using machinery in a factory.
重點記憶點: DSE閱讀中工業、製造業話題常考，表示「大量機器批量生產」，對應手工生產 hand-made。快時尚行業就是mass-produced clothes。
建議用法: 通常放在名詞前面：mass-produced clothes, mass-produced goods.

B1 / B2 (例句):Fast fashion describes cheap, mass-produced clothes that copy the latest runway trends and flood high-street shops every week. (快時尚就是廉價批量生產的衣服，複製最新伸展台潮流，每周湧入高街商店。)
詞組搭配 (Collocations): mass-produced clothing, mass-produced goods, mass-produced products

同義詞 (Synonyms): mass-market, quantity-produced
反義詞 (Antonyms): hand-made, custom-made

### garment  (n) <-- Added on 2026-07-09
文章分類：general
解釋: 衣服；成衣

**[深度解析 & 語境應用]**
英文定義: A piece of clothing.
重點記憶點: DSE閱讀時裝話題必考詞，比 clothes 更正式，工廠、製造業話題中常用 garment factory/industry。
建議用法: 可數名詞：a garment, the garment industry.

B1 / B2 (例句):The 2013 Rana Plaza factory collapse in Bangladesh killed over 1,100 garment workers. (2013年孟加拉國Rana Plaza廠房倒塌，超過1100名製衣工人死亡。)
詞組搭配 (Collocations): garment worker, garment factory, garment industry

同義詞 (Synonyms): clothing, apparel
反義詞 (Antonyms):—

### invisible  (adj) <-- Added on 2026-07-09
文章分類：general
解釋: 看不見的；無形的

**[深度解析 & 語境應用]**
英文定義: Impossible to see; not visible to the eye.
重點記憶點: DSE閱讀常考，invisible social pressure 就是「看不見的社會壓力」，形容人們不知不覺受到影響。
建議用法: 放在名詞前面：invisible pressure, invisible hand.

B1 / B2 (例句):Many people feel invisible social pressure from social media to buy new outfits for photos. (很多人感受到來自社交媒體看不見的社會壓力，要買新衣服拍照。)
詞組搭配 (Collocations): invisible pressure, invisible hand, invisible to the naked eye

同義詞 (Synonyms): unseen, hidden
反義詞 (Antonyms): visible, obvious

### business  (n) <-- Added on 2026-07-09
文章分類：general
解釋: 商業；生意；企業

**[深度解析 & 語境應用]**
英文定義: The activity of buying and selling goods and services; a company or organization that sells goods or services.
重點記憶點: 最常见的词，但DSE中常考 "business model" 表示「商业模式」，"business owner" 就是「企业所有者」。
建議用法: 可数指企业，不可数指商业活动：a business, do business with.

B1 / B2 (例句):This business model boomed in the 1960s, after factories gained fast sewing technology. (这个商业模式在1960年代迅速发展，工厂获得了快速缝纫技术。)
詞組搭配 (Collocations): business model, business owner, do business, small business

同義詞 (Synonyms): trade, commerce, company
反義詞 (Antonyms):—

### avoid  (v) <-- Added on 2026-07-09
文章分類：general
解釋: 避免；避开；逃避

**[深度解析 & 語境應用]**
英文定義: To stay away from a person, thing, or situation that you do not want.
重點記憶點: DSE阅读写作高频词，固定搭配 avoid doing something 「避免做某事」。
建議用法: 后面接动名词：avoid doing something.

B1 / B2 (例句):Many people avoid repeating the same clothes online, even if the old items are still in perfect condition. (很多人避免在網上重複穿同一件衣服，即使舊衣服仍然完好。)
詞組搭配 (Collocations): avoid doing something, avoid danger, avoid problems

同義詞 (Synonyms): evade, steer clear of
反義詞 (Antonyms): confront, face

### decades  (n (plural)) <-- Added on 2026-07-09
文章分類：general
解釋: 十年；數十年

**[深度解析 & 語境應用]**
英文定義: A period of ten years, especially a period of ten years starting with a year ending in 0.
重點記憶點: DSE中常用来表示「数十年的时间跨度」，for decades 就是「几十年來」。
建議用法: always plural: for decades, over three decades.

B1 / B2 (例句):Synthetic fabrics do not break down easily, so millions of unwanted clothes end up in landfills where they take up to 200 decades to decompose. (合成纤维不易分解，所以数百万件不需要的衣服最终进入垃圾填埋场，需要2000年才能分解。)
詞組搭配 (Collocations): for decades, over decades, several decades

同義詞 (Synonyms): ten-year periods
反義詞 (Antonyms):—

### competitors  (n (plural)) <-- Added on 2026-07-09
文章分類：general
解釋: 競爭對手；競爭者

**[深度解析 & 語境應用]**
英文定義: A person or company that competes against another person or company in the same market.
重點記憶點: DSE经济、商业话题常考，单数 competitor，复数 competitors。指同市场上其他竞争的企业或个人。
建議用法: plural form when talking about more than one.

B1 / B2 (例句):Companies chase the cheapest needle, meaning they constantly hunt manufacturers who can cut costs lower than competitors. (企业追求最便宜的生产成本，不断寻找能比竞争对手成本更低的制造商。)
詞組搭配 (Collocations): beat competitors, against competitors, lower costs than competitors

同義詞 (Synonyms): rivals, opponents
反義詞 (Antonyms): allies, partners
