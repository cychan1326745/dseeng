2 Multiple Choice 1 mark

Answer: A — underground nesting tunnels dug into cliff soil

Burrows are the underground tunnels puffins dig into soft cliff soil for nesting.

Burrows 指海鸚在懸崖軟土中挖掘的地下巢穴隧道。
